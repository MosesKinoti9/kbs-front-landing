About Our Company

Quality, Secure and Reliable Transport Services for all Kenyans!

Our core objective is to be the leading franchise Company that facilitates and provides safe, accessible, sustainable and efficient transport services that promote economic prosperity and enhance the lives of all Kenyans.

Our mission is to develop, promote and provide professional, equitable and sustainable transport services to all our customers.

Our vision is to be the most professional transport services provider in the jurisdiction we operate and beyond.

We also have put in all the appropriate COVID-19 protcols that are needed to ensure our customers' health and safety.
